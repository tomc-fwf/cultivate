III. Watering Discipline

DO

  • Small rings around root zone
  • Wait until pot is light, not bone-dry (~30% of saturated weight)
  • Encourage oxygen exchange in peat
  • Lift pot before watering — judge by weight

DON'T

  • Saturate the entire pot every feed
  • Chase runoff at this stage
  • Let Pro-Mix HP fully dry out (becomes hydrophobic)
  • Water on a fixed schedule — read the plant

IV. Plant Reading

Healthy Signs

  • Moderate matte green
  • Leaves praying upward
  • Fast node development
  • Tight internodes
  • Rapid root expansion

Warning Signs

  • Dark shiny / waxy leaves
  • Clawing leaf tips
  • Burnt tips → back off N
  • Slow / stalled growth
  • Wet droopy appearance

VI. Organics Alive Notes

Days 14–21: Run at 25–33% of label strength. Introduce on day 14 at the low end and only step up if plants look vigorous and unstressed. Biology > EC at this stage — you're feeding the microbes, the microbes feed the plant.

By Day 21 You Want

Fully rooted 4" pots
Tight internodes
4–6+ nodes developing
No major stress events
Strong transplant readiness
The goal is uninterrupted momentum, not maximum feed strength.
